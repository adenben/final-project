Research-Based Project: Student Internship Guide

for Current Issues in Education Capstone (EDUC696)
at Liberty University (October 2019)

The goal of this project was to present research in a tangible format to assist students on the journey of successfully completing an internship. The guide was intended to assist Old Dominion University students from the beginning of internship searching, leading to performance as an intern, and how to capitalize on the experience.
