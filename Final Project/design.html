Design Portfolio

Website Redesign: Dunder Mifflin Paper Company

for Digital Imagery in Web Design (VIC5325)
at University of Florida (December 2020)

This project was intended to allow the student to think of digital imagery from a UI/UX perspective and apply graphics using Adobe Photoshop to multiple screen sizes while retaining visual continuity.

Social Media Campaign: Travel Vida

for Digital Imagery in Web Design (VIC5325)
at University of Florida (November 2020)

This project allowed students to use the technical and compositional skills learned to create an overall look and feel for the campaign and apply it to multiple ads for Instragram, Facebook, and Twitter.

Email Blast: Summer Adoption Event

for Digital Imagery in Web Design (VIC5325)
at University of Florida (October 2020)

The task for this project was to design an email blast for a non-profit animal rescue group advertising their upcoming adoption event. Students were given some assets and parameters that were required to include, such as branding, size, and copy.
