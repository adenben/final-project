About me!

Hi! I'm Anissa (pronounced Ah-nee-sa).

During my 8-5 job I have pleasure of preparing students for their future career goals. Outside of my 8-5, I'm a web design student at the University of Florida. My interests span across different areas. I was even a music production major at one point!

Feel free to follow my path of twists and turns below.
